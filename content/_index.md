title: "The Scaffold Rack"
description: "A hands-on platform for building and operating a modern homelab — documented publicly at thescaffoldrack.com"
---

This is a ground-up rebuild of a homelab into platform-engineered
infrastructure, documented publicly as it happens. Honest about what works,
what doesn't, and why.

Scaffolding is temporary and iterative by nature — the name signals building
in public, not a finished reference architecture. Expect rough edges,
reversed decisions, and the occasional dead end. That's the point.
